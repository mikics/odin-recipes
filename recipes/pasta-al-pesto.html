Pasta al pesto

Description

Pasta al pesto is a typical Italian dish, originating from Liguria. It is made with pasta and pesto sauce, which is made with basil, european pine nuts, parmesan cheese, oil and (sometimes) garlic.

Ingredients (4 people)

  • 400g pasta
  • 1 cup basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup pine nuts
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic
  1. Boil the pasta in salted water
  2. Make the pesto sauce by mixing the basil, pine nuts, parmesan cheese, olive oil and garlic in a mortar with a pestle, or in a blender
  3. Drain the pasta when cooked, and mix it with the pesto sauce
  4. Enjoy!
